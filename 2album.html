Home: Confinement // Comfort

A photo project that reflects the oscillating sentiments of having my movement limited, and investigates the meaning of "home," which in my pandemic experience has proved to be both a space of confinement and comfort.

Seoul Engagement

An installation of photographs inspired by Tokyoite and Seoulite 90's archives, that follows a typical day of urban exploration and a literal interpretation of how I "engage with the city" admist a different pandemic reality within a shared global condition.
